How It Works

1

Wind Rotates Turbine

Natural wind energy spins the turbine blades, converting kinetic energy into rotational motion.

2

Smart controllers optimize voltage and current for safe, efficient power delivery to your home or battery.

3

Clean electricity powers your appliances directly or charges batteries for later use.
